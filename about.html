Who we are & What we do

Neosho Security Solutions is a premium, client-focused security installation and maintenance provider operating in Johannesburg, Roodepoort, and surrounding areas. We specialize in providing custom-engineered physical and electronic security systems that safeguard homes, estates, and business premises.

We design, install, repair, and maintain complete electronic security infrastructures. From high-definition CCTV arrays and multi-tenant access control networks to gate motors, alarms, intercoms, and electric fencing, we ensure every detail of your security setup operates reliably 24/7.

Residential Security

Our residential security services focus on keeping your family and estate safe. We design systems that fit your lifestyle, using reliable perimeter barriers like electric fencing, gate automation, and smart wireless burglar alarms that can be monitored remotely from your mobile device.

Commercial Security

For commercial buildings and complexes, we focus on access control, asset protection, and detailed site visibility. We offer biometric entry controls, vehicle gate barriers, high-resolution IP camera surveillance grids, and structured maintenance support that prevents security breakdowns.

Trusted security product support

We believe a security system is only as strong as its components. That is why Neosho Security Solutions works exclusively with industry-standard, reputable security manufacturers.

By supporting brands like Centurion Systems, Hansa, Paradox, IDS, DSC, Hikvision, and Dahua, we guarantee that replacement parts are readily available, equipment warranties are fully supported, and software integrations remain updated and secure.
